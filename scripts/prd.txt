# Product Requirements Document: AI-Powered Legal Case Management System

## 1. Introduction
This document outlines the requirements for an AI-Powered Legal Case Management System (hereinafter referred to as "Better Case App"). The system aims to provide lawyers and legal professionals with an intelligent, efficient, and streamlined platform to manage their cases, leveraging AI technologies for enhanced productivity and insights.

## 2. Goals
- To simplify and automate routine case management tasks.
- To provide powerful AI-driven tools for legal research, document analysis, and drafting.
- To centralize case information, making it easily accessible and searchable.
- To improve collaboration among legal teams.
- To enhance decision-making through data-driven insights and AI assistance.

## 3. Target Users
- Solo legal practitioners
- Lawyers in small to medium-sized law firms
- In-house legal counsel
- Paralegals and legal assistants

## 4. Key Features

### 4.1. Core Case Management
- **Create, Read, Update, Delete (CRUD) Cases:**
    - Case details: name, client, area of law, description, status (e.g., open, pending, closed), key dates, assigned lawyer(s).
    - Ability to link related cases.
- **Client Management:**
    - Store client information (contact details, case history).
    - Link clients to their respective cases.
- **Area of Law & Country Management:**
    - Predefined and customizable lists for areas of law and relevant jurisdictions/countries.

### 4.2. AI-Powered Chatbot
- **Legal Research Assistance:**
    - Natural language queries to find relevant statutes, case law, and legal articles.
    - Integration with legal databases (potentially via APIs or indexed content).
- **Case Summarization:**
    - Generate summaries of case files or specific documents.
- **Document Drafting Assistance:**
    - Provide templates and suggestions for common legal documents.
    - Help draft clauses or sections based on user input.
- **Q&A on Case Details:**
    - Answer questions about specific cases based on the information stored in the system.

### 4.3. Elasticsearch Integration
- **Advanced Search:**
    - Full-text search across all case data, including notes, documents, and metadata.
    - Faceted search and filtering (e.g., by client, date, area of law, status).
- **Document Indexing:**
    - Index uploaded documents (PDFs, Word files, etc.) for comprehensive search.
- **Legal Suggestion Engine:**
    - Surface relevant internal case information, precedents, or linked law articles based on current case context or query.
    - Display snippets, relevance scores, and links to full text.

### 4.4. Document Management
- **Secure Document Upload & Storage:**
    - Attach documents to specific cases.
    - Version control for documents.
- **Optical Character Recognition (OCR):**
    - Make scanned documents searchable.

### 4.5. User Management & Security
- **Authentication & Authorization:**
    - Role-based access control (e.g., admin, lawyer, paralegal).
    - Secure login mechanisms.
- **Data Privacy & Compliance:**
    - Features to support compliance with relevant data protection regulations (e.g., GDPR, CCPA).

### 4.6. Task Management & Calendar (Future Considerations)
- **Task Assignment:** Assign tasks related to cases to team members.
- **Deadline Tracking:** Set and manage deadlines for cases and tasks.
- **Calendar Integration:** View case-related events and deadlines.

## 5. Technical Considerations
- **Backend:** Node.js, tRPC, Prisma (for database interaction)
- **Database:** Relational database (e.g., PostgreSQL)
- **Search:** Elasticsearch
- **Frontend:** Web-based (e.g., Next.js, React)
- **AI:** Integration with LLMs for chatbot and other AI features.

## 6. Success Metrics
- User adoption rate and daily/monthly active users.
- Average time spent per user in the application.
- Reduction in time spent on manual case management tasks (user-reported).
- Number of AI-assisted searches/queries performed.
- User satisfaction scores (e.g., NPS).
